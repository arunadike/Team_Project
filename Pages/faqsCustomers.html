Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get asked about our travel packages. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us.

What types of travel packages are offered?
We offer a variety of travel packages, including flights, hotels, and activities. Travel agents can customize packages with specific pricing and itinerary details.
How do I book a travel package?
Customers can search for available packages, select their desired option, and proceed to secure payment processing to confirm their booking.
What payment methods are accepted?
We integrate with multiple payment gateways to offer a variety of secure payment methods.
Can I rate and review a travel package?
Yes, customers can rate travel packages and provide feedback after their trip. Travel agents can also respond to these reviews.
Is travel insurance available?
Yes, customers can opt for travel insurance during the booking process.
What kind of support is provided?
We offer 24/7 travel assistance and emergency support.
How can I manage my booking?
Users can manage their bookings through their account dashboard. Specific functionalities will be available in future updates.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, customers can cancel bookings up to 7 days before departure.
