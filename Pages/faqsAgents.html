Frequently Asked Questions for Travel Agents

Here are some of the most common questions we get asked about our platform from Travel Agents.

How do I create a travel package?
Travel agents can create travel packages by providing details such as the title, description, duration, price, and included services. You can also specify the itinerary, including flights, hotels, and sightseeing.
How do I manage bookings for my packages?
When a customer books a package, you will be notified with the booking details, including customer information, package details, and booking dates. The system will also manage booking status.
Can I respond to customer reviews?
Yes, you can respond to customer reviews and feedback for your travel packages. This helps you engage with your customers and address any concerns they may have.
How do I get paid?
Payments from customers are processed securely through our integrated payment gateways. Details of the payment method and status are recorded and linked to the booking.
What support is available for travel agents?
We offer 24/7 travel assistance and emergency support.
What are the requirements to list packages?
Travel agents must verify their identity before listing packages.
